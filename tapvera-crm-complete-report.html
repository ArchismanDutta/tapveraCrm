  • Email & password authentication with password visibility toggle
  • Role-based automatic routing — employees go to employee dashboard, admins to the HR suite

Security meets simplicity. Employees don't need IT support to get in — they just type their email, enter their password, and they're home.

A fully automated time-tracking system that records work hours, breaks, and arrival times with precision and zero paperwork.

The Story

Time tracking used to mean paper registers, manual entries, and disputes at month-end. Not anymore. When an employee clicks "Punch In," a live work timer starts. The system captures their exact arrival time, tracks work duration to the second, and manages every break they take — all automatically.

The "Today's Timeline" shows a chronological log of every punch-in and punch-out event. The Week Summary on the right gives the employee a complete picture of their total hours, average daily work, break time, and on-time rate — empowering them to self-manage.

  • One-click Punch In with instant confirmation notification
  • Live work duration counter — tracks hours, minutes, and seconds in real time
  • Break Management with three types: Lunch, Coffee, and Personal
  • Arrival Time recorded and displayed (e.g., "Arrived: 09:04 AM")
  • Today's Timeline showing full punch history for the day
  • Week Summary: Total Hours, Avg Daily Work, Total Break, On-Time Rate, Breaks Taken
  • Flexible Shift Request option for employees needing schedule changes
  • Real-time status indicator showing "Currently Working" or break type

No more disputes. No more guesswork. Employees and managers both have access to the same precise, timestamped record — transparency that builds trust on both sides.
