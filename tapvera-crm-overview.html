One platform that runs your entire business.

Tapvera CRM is a custom-built business management system designed specifically for your company. It replaces the need for multiple disconnected tools — spreadsheets, WhatsApp groups, separate payroll software, and manual attendance registers.

Everything — your employees, clients, projects, payments, and leads — lives in one place. Your team logs in, does their work, and you get a clear view of everything happening across the business.

Think of it as the central nervous system of your company. Whether you are managing five people or five hundred, Tapvera CRM gives you the visibility and control to keep things running smoothly — without the chaos.

  • Attendance registers → Digital check-in with real-time tracking

Access & Security

The right people see the right things.

Not everyone in your company needs access to everything. Tapvera has four built-in user roles that control exactly what each person can see and do.

Super Admin
Full access to everything. Manages all settings, all employees, all data, and all configurations. Typically the business owner.
Admin
Manages day-to-day operations — attendance, payroll, projects, and leads. Broad access within the boundaries set by the Super Admin.
HR
Focused on employee-related features — attendance, leaves, payroll, and positions. No access to sales, project financials, or system settings.
Employee
Sees only their own data — their attendance, payslips, tasks, and projects they are part of. Simple, focused, and distraction-free.
