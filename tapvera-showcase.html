2Employee Portal · Feature 2
Punch In / Punch Out — Every Minute, Tracked Automatically
A fully digital time-tracking system replacing paper registers with live, precise, tamper-proof records.

The moment an employee clicks "Punch In," a live work timer begins counting. The system records the exact arrival time down to the second and begins tracking work duration in real time. The screen shows Work Duration, Break Time, and Arrival Time — all live. No manual entries, no approximations, no disputes at month-end. Both the employee and their manager see the same precise, timestamped record.

Break Management lets employees categorize their breaks — Lunch, Coffee, or Personal — keeping work and break time clearly separated. The "Today's Timeline" shows a chronological log of every event for the day. The Week Summary panel on the right gives a full picture: Total Hours worked this week, Average Daily Work, Total Break time, On-Time Rate, and Breaks Taken. Employees can also request a Flexible Shift if their schedule needs to change — the request goes to admin for approval.
