### How It Works:
1. Files are uploaded to S3 via multer-s3
2. S3 URLs are converted to CloudFront URLs for CDN delivery
3. CloudFront provides:
   - Faster global delivery
   - SSL/HTTPS by default
   - Caching for better performance
   - Lower bandwidth costs
